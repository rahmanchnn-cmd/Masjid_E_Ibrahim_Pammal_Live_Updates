How to connect your Google Sheet:

  1. Open your Google Sheet containing the dashboard data.
  2. Ensure the sheets are named exactly: "Prayer Timings", "Jumuah Katheeb", "Weekly Programs", "Monthly Programs", "Upcoming Events", "Past program history", and "Reports".
  3. Click Share in the top-right corner of Google Sheets.
  4. Under General Access, change "Restricted" to "Anyone with the link can view" (Viewer permission is enough!).
  5. Copy the sheet URL and paste it into the field above.
  6. Click Save & Connect. The dashboard will automatically pull and render the sheet data!
